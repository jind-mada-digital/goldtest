Goldtest of Switzerland is a brand of SSSV-Boutique-AESS. Our company based in Torgon (VS) was founded in 1991 by a former ski school director, with the support of the Swiss Ski School Association. Our goal is to develop high quality clothing, personalized outfits, course and training materials and other products for winter sports. 

As an official supplier of the Swiss Ski Schools, we work every day on the ideal winter sports equipment. And everything else that is needed for professional skiing. From high-quality clothing to versatile accessories. 

Our two Goldtest clothing lines are aimed at professional and private skiers. The League5 collection is designed to meet the demands of ski school instructors. The Club5 collection is designed for individuals with the same requirements for their ski clothing.

In addition, our other service "Easy Ski School" provides ski schools with all the necessary equipment for lessons, competitions and events. The products can be personalized and used for different occasions. They are mainly intended for ski schools, but also for other branches, events and individual customers.
